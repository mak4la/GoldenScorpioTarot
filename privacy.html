Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Golden Scorpio Tarot respects your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected when you request or purchase a tarot reading, how it is used, and the choices you have.

Information Collected

When you use this website, Golden Scorpio Tarot may collect:

  • Your email address.
  • The type of tarot reading you request.
  • Any focus area, details, birth information, or context you choose to submit.
  • Payment confirmation and basic order details from Stripe.

Golden Scorpio Tarot does not collect or store your full card number. Payments are processed by Stripe, and Stripe handles payment information according to its own privacy and security practices.

How Information Is Used

Your information is used to:

  • Respond to your request and provide your tarot reading.
  • Confirm purchases, payments, and reading details.
  • Contact you about your order if more information is needed.
  • Maintain basic business records.

Sharing Information

Golden Scorpio Tarot does not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only with service providers needed to operate the website, receive form submissions, process payments, or comply with legal requirements.

Third-Party Services

This website may use Formspree to receive booking inquiries and Stripe to process payments. Those services may collect information according to their own policies.

Tarot Reading Details

Please only submit information you are comfortable sharing. Tarot readings are for personal reflection and entertainment, and you should avoid submitting sensitive medical, legal, financial, or emergency information through the form.

Data Retention

Request and order information may be kept as long as needed to provide readings, respond to questions, keep business records, and meet legal or tax obligations.

Your Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information you have submitted by contacting Golden Scorpio Tarot at goldenscorpiotarot@gmail.com.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new last updated date.
